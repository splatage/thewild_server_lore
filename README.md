# The Wild Library Commands

This document collects two early library collections for **The Wild** in Minecraft Java's modern written-book data-component style.

## Shelf 1 — Papers Kept Beside the Old Sanctuary

**Location:** the small camp beside the ruins of the Old Sanctuary at player spawn.

**Tone and role:** a recovered run of personal journals by Mara Fen, one of the original settlers who stayed at the center while others moved outward. These books quietly teach the player that the center is safer, the holdings are scattered, the shop binds them together, lean times are real, and the Wild beyond civilization is dangerous and rich.

### Book 1. First Sight of the Sanctuary
- **Author:** Mara Fen
- **Genre:** Camp journal
- **Lore purpose:** Finding the Old Sanctuary and choosing to hold the center.

```mcfunction
/give @p written_book[minecraft:written_book_content={title:{raw:"First Sight of the Sanctuary"},author:"Mara Fen",generation:0,resolved:true,pages:[{text:"We came on the ruin by late light, with just enough sun left to see that one wall still held and part of the roof had not yet given itself entirely to weather."},{text:"Someone called it the Old Sanctuary. The name felt too large for what remained, yet not wrong."},{text:"The others spoke of pressing on in the morning and finding better ground farther out. I did not argue that better ground may exist."},{text:"I only said that good ground is no use to tired hands if they have no sure fire, no dry stores, and no place to return to."},{text:"So we made camp beside the broken stones. We cleared ash from the old hearth, set our packs under cover, and marked a shelf for papers and tallies."},{text:"It was little enough, but little things are what keep a place from becoming wild again overnight."},{text:"I think now that the realm did not begin here because this was the finest place."},{text:"It began here because it was a place we could hold.\n\nThere is wisdom in a center that survives."}]}]

## Shelf 2 — The Return and First Holdings

**Location:** the first full shelf in the main library.

**Layout:** 27 slots, arranged as a mixed civic archive.

**Genre mix:** chronicle entries, fragments, settlement registers, survey books, roadwarden logs, quartermaster papers, letters, inventories, memoranda, honor rolls, and librarian notes.

**Lore role:** this shelf widens the world from one camp to a growing realm. It records the Return, the first outward holdings, the establishment of the shop, the necessity of roads and depots, lean seasons, memory, distinction, and the earliest shapers later remembered as the Primordials.

- **Author:** Aster Vale
- **Genre:** Chronicle entry
- **Lore purpose:** Sets the age of rebuilding after retreat and loss.

```mcfunction

We came into a land half-held, half-lost, where old crossings had gone to rot and memory outlasted walls only by stubbornness."},{text:"Some speak of founding as if all things begin fresh.
